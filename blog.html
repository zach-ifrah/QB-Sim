WHAT IS THE GAMEPLAY LIKE?

Which platforms and consoles can I play it on...April 19th, 2023

Released in Canada, QB simulator is a 3rd person quarter-back target game. The main goal of our game is to hit targets with one of our QBs. As you progress in the game, the levels will increase and so will the difficulty. This will mean that possibly you are met with someone on defense who you must get by to hit the target.

When the levels increase, the targets may begin to move to up the difficulty of the game.

With diverse levels, stadiums and difficulties, this video game is for everyone! We truly hope you enjoy our creation.

WHAT IS THIS GAME BASED OFF OF?

Well, it's based off all football fans' biggest dream...April 17th 2023

To young, middle aged, or old football fans, you must have all thought at least once what life would be like if you made the team which included the best of the best - the cream of the crop.

You most likely know what we're talking about. Nevertheless, if you don't, we are speaking of the National Football League, or the NFL. Our video game, QB simulator, is certainly inspired by the NFL and the sport of football as a whole. However, we knew that there wasn't a game out there that portrayed solely being a QB so we took inspiration to make a video game dedicated to this.
