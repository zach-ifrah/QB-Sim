What is QB Simulator?

In this brand new video game, you will experience first hand what it's like to be a football quarter-back - but in a game!

Released in Canada, QB simulator is a 3rd person role player quarter-back target game. The main goal of our game is to hit targets with one of our QBs. As you progress in the game, the levels will increase and so will the difficulty. This will mean that possibly you are met with someone on defense who you must get by to hit the target.

When the levels increase, the targets may begin to move to up the difficulty of the game.

With diverse levels, stadiums and difficulties, this video game is for everyone! We truly hope you enjoy our creation.
